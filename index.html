What We Do

Specialized Surface Restoration

We engineer specific pressure and chemical ratios tailored to your property's masonry, roofing, and structural layout.

Driveway Restoration

Vehicular traffic quickly tracks oil, tire marks, and deep-seated grime into porous paving and concrete. Utilizing precision pressure systems, we extract embedded pollutants and tough stains – safely restoring immaculate curb appeal while preventing the long-term degradation of your stone surfaces.

Paving Restoration

South Africa's intense summer heat and heavy downpours create the perfect breeding ground for deep-seated grime, stubborn moss, and unsightly black algae on porous paving that a standard hose pipe can't touch. Our specialized deep-clean systems penetrate deep into the paver's surface, extracting embedded pollutants and eliminating slick moss at the root – instantly restoring your driveway or entertainment patio to its original, pristine look.

Low-Pressure Roof Washing

High pressure can permanently destroy roof tiles and exterior plaster coatings. We utilize specialized soft-wash chemical systems to safely kill algae, mould, and moss at the root, preserving your structural integrity and extending your paintwork's lifespan.

Commercial & HOA Facility Maintenance

High-traffic commercial properties demand consistent safety and cleanliness compliance. We provide scheduled, recurring maintenance programs for retail strips, office parks, and residential estates – focusing on dumpster pads, drive-thrus, parking structures, and boundary walls.
