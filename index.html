What We Do

Specialized Surface Restoration

We engineer specific pressure and chemical ratios tailored to your property's masonry, roofing, and structural layout.

Driveway Restoration

Vehicular traffic quickly tracks oil, tire marks, and deep-seated grime into porous paving and concrete. Utilizing precision pressure systems, we extract embedded pollutants and tough stains – safely restoring immaculate curb appeal while preventing the long-term degradation of your stone surfaces.

Paving Restoration

South Africa's intense summer heat and heavy downpours create the perfect breeding ground for deep-seated grime, stubborn moss, and unsightly black algae on porous paving that a standard hose pipe can't touch. Our specialized deep-clean systems penetrate deep into the paver's surface, extracting embedded pollutants and eliminating slick moss at the root – instantly restoring your driveway or entertainment patio to its original, pristine look.

Low-Pressure Roof Washing

High pressure can permanently destroy roof tiles and exterior plaster coatings. We utilize specialized soft-wash chemical systems to safely kill algae, mould, and moss at the root, preserving your structural integrity and extending your paintwork's lifespan.

Commercial & HOA Facility Maintenance

High-traffic commercial properties demand consistent safety and cleanliness compliance. We provide scheduled, recurring maintenance programs for retail strips, office parks, and residential estates – focusing on dumpster pads, drive-thrus, parking structures, and boundary walls.

MODERN PRESSURE WASH (A Division of Modern Renovator)
Last Updated: June 2026

At Modern Pressure Wash, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in strict accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), No. 4 of 2013, and other applicable South African data protection legislation.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, utilize our services, or engage with our digital subscription channels.

01

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that is relevant and necessary for providing our property preservation and surface restoration services. This includes:

  • Identifiable Information: Name, surname, company name (if applicable), registration numbers, and job titles (for commercial entities).
  • Contact Information: Email address, mobile numbers, and WhatsApp contact details.
  • Property Information: Physical addresses of residential properties or commercial facilities requiring assessment, layout descriptions, structural details, and property photos uploaded via our intake forms.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, cookies, and usage data gathered automatically when you browse our website.
02

How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is processed lawfully and transparently for specific business operations, including:

  • Formulating highly accurate, itemized project proposals and Service Level Agreements (SLAs).
  • Scheduling technical teams, executing precision cleaning operations, and conducting quality sign-offs.
  • Processing secure monthly subscription billings through our authorized financial merchant gateway (PayFast).
  • Sending automated service reminders, property updates, and before-and-after operational reports.
03

Data Retention and Destruction

  • Retention Period: We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the operational purposes outlined in this policy, or to comply with statutory financial and legal record-keeping requirements in South Africa.
  • Secure Destruction: Once your data is no longer required or your service contract ends, all physical documents are securely shredded, and digital records are permanently deleted using secure data-erasure methodologies.
04

Third-Party Disclosures (PayFast & Google)

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. To execute our services efficiently, we share limited, necessary data with trusted operators:

05

Your Lawful Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject in South Africa, you possess the right to:

  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal information at any time for direct marketing purposes.
  • Deletion: Request the permanent deletion or destruction of your personal profile from our communication systems.

We implement robust technical and organizational security protocols to prevent unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration of your personal records. Our digital framework relies on secure cloud encryption, password-protected databases, and restricted employee data privileges.
